Caramel Macchiato
Scores of people are passionate devotees of this signature beverage. So bewitched are they, you’d think it was some kind of magical elixir. We’ll tell you exactly what goes into it: creamy vanilla-flavored syrup, freshly steamed milk with a topping of velvety-rich foam, an intense hit of our Espresso Roast, a finishing of buttery caramel drizzle.
